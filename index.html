Jessica Hyman is currently Chief of Staff and the Project Management Officer for the Chief People Officer at Atlassian. Jessica is a former global operations lead specialising in corporate social responsibility. She's consulted on issues ranging from employee engagement, women's empowerment and collaborative office space design.
